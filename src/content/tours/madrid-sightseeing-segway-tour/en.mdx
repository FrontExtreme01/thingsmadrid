<h3 class="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold text-sky-800"> Essential Madrid (60 minutes tour)</h3>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Meet your guide at our office in Calle Escalinata number 10 (Plaza de la Opera). The first stops will be 
at Plaza de Santiago and Plaza de Ramales, from where you will slide towards Plaza de Oriente. See the impressive Royal Palace and the beautiful 
Almudena Cathedral. Glide down Mayor Street and learn about the city history in this itinerary that will take you to the main squares in town. 
Admire the gastronomic temple the Mercado de San Miguel is and reach the Plaza Mayor. Continue to Puerta del Sol and see the emblematic Spanish 
“Km0” and the statue of the Oso and the Madrone, symbol of the city. The itinerary finishes at the same departure point. </p>

<h3 class="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold text-sky-800"> Magic Retiro (60 minutes tour)</h3>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> The most magical tour of the Retiro Park, ideal for families. First of all, we will visit the ruins of 
the Church of San Isidoro, the “Casita del Pescador” and the “Casa de Fieras”, the main building of the old zoo of Madrid converted 10 years ago 
into a library. We will continue to the Cecilio Rodriguez gardens and visit the statue of the Fallen Angel, one of the few monuments to the devil 
in the world, which is located 666 metres above sea level. We will continue to one of the most spectacular monuments in the park, the Crystal Palace. 
In the last part of the visit we will discover the popular pond, where the monument to King Alfonso XII is located. We will visit one of the most 
emblematic monuments of Madrid, the "Puerta de Alcalá" and we will see the oldest tree in Madrid. The tour ends at the same place of departure. </p>

<h3 class="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold text-sky-800"> Madrid Downtown (90 minutes tour) - Best Seller</h3>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Discover Madrid in depth with this tour. Leave our office in Calle Escalinata number 10 and head towards the 
Royal Palace and the Almudena Cathedral. Continue to Casa de Campo, the largest urban park in Spain and see its lake and historic Huerta de la Partida. 
Glide along the Manzanares River and its three historic bridges. Come back downtown through Atens Park and Cuesta de la Vega and enter the historic 
part of town. See emblematic areas such as Calle Mayor, Plaza de la Villa, the Mercado de San Miguel and Puerta del Sol. The tour finishes at the same 
departure point </p>

<h3 class="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold text-sky-800"> Madrid with a tapa (150 minutes tour)</h3>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Leave our office in Calle Escalinata number 10 and head to the majestic Royal Palace. Visit Casa de Campo, 
the largest urban park in Spain through Cuesta de la Vega. Slide along the Manzanares River and see its ancient bridges. Continue to reach the prestigious 
Pasarela de Arganzuela and the artificial beaches Madrid has. </p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Come back into the city center and enter the historic part of town. See emblematic areas such as Calle Mayor, 
Plaza de la Villa, the Mercado de San Miguel and Puerta del Sol. There, you will be able to admire the well-known city symbol, the Bear and the Madrone. 
This route finishes with a paella tapa at a restaurant located 1 minute walking distance from the departure point. </p>
