<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Check in at our office and join us on the first part of the tour, a one hour walking tour through the 
streets of the historic city center of Madrid. Discover emblematic places you cannot miss! Stroll around Plaza de la Villa, the famous Plaza 
Mayor, Puerta del Sol and Plaza de Oriente. Then board one of our air-conditioned buses for a ride through the city. Remember to bring your 
camera or Smartphone! </p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Leave Plaza de Oriente and head to the magnificent Puerta de Toledo. Did you know its construction dates back 
from the Napoleonic era? See the oldest bridge in Madrid and drive next to the Manzanares River, from where you will have great views of the Royal 
Palace and the Almudena Cathedral. Pass by Mayor Street, Puerta del Sol square and the Las Cortes quarter on your way to the Paseo del Prado. Admire 
the architecture of the Old Madrid arriving into the Las Letras Quarter.</p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Drive by Cibeles Fountain, where Real Madrid fans celebrate the team victories. Do not miss Puerta de Alcalá, 
the most famous of the five ancient gates into the city. The last sights you will see on the tour are the Santiago Bernabéu stadium through Paseo de 
la Castellana street and Colon square.</p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Next on schedule is your self-guided tour to the home to Real Madrid CF, this visit will not leave any soccer 
fan indifferent. Enjoy a tour of the facilities in the heart of Madrid, where you can learn and recall the history of hundreds of priceless objects, 
including the most prestigious trophies won by Real Madrid, the best club of the 20th century. <b>The tour includes:</b></p>

<ul class="md:ml-10 text-lg text-justify space-y-4 mb-4">
    <li><i class="at-check-circle text-green-500"></i> Panoramic view of the stadium, the visit begins in style.</li>

</ul>
