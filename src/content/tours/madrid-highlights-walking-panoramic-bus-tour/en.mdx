<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Check in at our office and join us on the first part of the tour, a one hour walking tour through the streets

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Leave Plaza de Oriente and head to the magnificent Puerta de Toledo. Did you know its construction dates back

Palace and the Almudena Cathedral. Pass by Mayor Street, Puerta del Sol square and the Las Cortes quarter on your way to the Paseo del Prado. Admire 
the architecture of the Old Madrid arriving into the Las Letras Quarter. </p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Drive by Cibeles Fountain, where Real Madrid fans celebrate the team victories. Do not miss Puerta de Alcalá,

la Castellana street and Colon square.</p>
