<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Enjoy your visit through the Royal Monastery of El Escorial, an architectural ensemble declared World 
Heritage Site by UNESCO. Built in the 16th century, it is a symbol of the power of the government at the time. Accompanied by your guide, you 
will discover the most important areas of the monument. Of Renaissance style, you will marvel at the building symmetry as you walk along the 
site visiting the Habsburg’s Palace, the Chapter House, the Basilica and the remarkable library.</p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Board your bus again for a short drive to the Valley of the Fallen. The site is located 9km away from 
El Escorial, in Sierra de Guadarrama. You will be able to see it from far away thanks to the monumental cross built above the site. The 
monument is an underground church built into the mountain. The same is topped by the World’s Largest Cross, which can be seen from miles away.</p>
