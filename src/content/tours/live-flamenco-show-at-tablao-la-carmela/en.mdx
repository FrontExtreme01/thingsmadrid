<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Enjoy the true art of flamenco at Tablao La Carmela. Located very close of Puerta del Sol, in 
the old coal cellars of the Taberna "La Carmela" <em>(former Convent of La Victoria, demolished in 1836 after the War of Independence)</em>. </p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> Every night the best dancers (known as bailaores) come to the Tablao La Carmela, who will make 
you enjoy this art in each of their shows.</p>

<p class="text-lg text-justify my-5"> If you choose the dinner option, you will be delighted with the best traditional gastronomy. With the 
"Picoteo Menu”, you can discover the flavours that reflects the past and present of the tablao while you enjoy the show.</p>
